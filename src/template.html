Someone stole my work!

Theft is very ICKY, and you can use copyright to get back what is yours!

Do note that copyright takedowns are a legal process, and as such you will need your legal information in many cases to make claims. You are to be submitting claims in good faith, entirely correct, and under penalty of perjury if false.

Some other questions

I got a false claim!

Support pages can have details about submitting counter-claims, and some just use the same method as a normal claim. You should be fast though about submitting the counter claim!

Perjury?

Since DMCA takedowns are a legal process, yes: if you lie in your takedown request you can be subject to the penalty of perjury. Further details are dependent on jurisdiction, but don't risk it.

Something missing/wrong? Make an issue on GitHub or even ask me nicely on Bluesky. In many cases searching "[service] copyright claim" in a search engine will give you what you need as well.
